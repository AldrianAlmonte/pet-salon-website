What you need to know about us!

We provide the highest quality of care to your pet and strive to achieve the results to your satisfaction. Our highly trained professionals bring years of experience to our business while providing compassion, patience, and safety to your pet. We strive to provide excellent customer service and a “paws”-ative experience for you and your pet.

Contact us for all your professional dog and cat grooming needs. And always feel free to contact us with any questions or comments.

Our VIP! Treatment Includes:

Soothing Baths

Does your dog have dry, itchy skin due to seasonal allergies? We offer a soothing oatmeal bath to help calm your dog’s skin. We also have a hypo allergenic shampoo for dogs with allergies and an allerpet shampoo for dogs to help owners who have allergies! In addition, we offer deep remoisturizing treatment for extreme dry and itchy skin that will leave your dog feeling calm and clean.

Scented & Special Baths

We offer a variety of scented baths, including, Pina Colada, Very Berry, Green Apple, Baby Powder, Rainforest, Spiced Apple and Prickly Pear. We also offer special whitening shampoo for white hair dogs and shampoo for black dogs to keep the coat looking smooth and conditioned. Ask about our holiday scents!

Furminator

By far the most popular product to reduce shedding 60-80% over a 4 to 6 week period. The Furminator system is NOT a chemical treatment. Both shampoo and conditioner contain natural ingredients designed to enhance the condition of the skin while reducing excessive shedding.

Designer Colognes

Treat your pet to a designer cologne after the bath to keep that clean, fresh scent. Available scents include: Arfmani, White Dalmatians, CK-9, Timmy Holedigger, Aramutts, Beautifur, Bono Sports, Fursions Light Blue and Polo.
